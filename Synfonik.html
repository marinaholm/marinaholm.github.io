Fanzine Synfonik

As part of a workshop led by Hadrien Herzog, we had to create a fanzine tackling the theme of urgency including one constraint which was printing in only one color. With my group we decided to highlight the committed singers that we chose like Cindy Lauper, Sam Cooke and the group Radiohead. Moreover, we wanted to question the classic layout. For us the creation of this fanzine was a way to explore our freedom regarding the layout and explore various things on InDesign and Photoshop. We also had the feeling that we wanted to create something spontaneous by mixing photos and texts : we played with the relationship between text and image.
